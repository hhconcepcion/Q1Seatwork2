Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Name of Character Description
Maria Clara María Clara is one of the main characters in Noli Me Tangere and is the daughter of Padre Dámaso and Doña Pía Alba. She is the lover of the main protagonist, Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin since childhood. She was also known for her beauty and decency. According to many, Maria Clara symbolizes our country, the Phillipines which is innocent, fragile and pure. Deguzman, J. (2020). Noli Me Tangere Characters. https://www.artstation.com/artwork/486myY
Don Rafael Don Rafael Ibarra was the father of Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin. He was best known for being the wealthiest man in San Digeo. Unfortunately, he died during the opening pages of Noli Me Tangere for he was accused of a few things. He was accused for killing someone and he was accused for choosing not to confess to the church. He was declared as an erehe and pilibustero. He died during his stay in jail. N/A. (2020). Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin is the main protagonist in Noli Me Tangere. He is the son of Don Rafael and was born in San Diego. Their family were known to be the wealthiest in San Digeo. He studied and lived half of his life in Spain. He is the childhood sweetheart of Maria Clara. He symbolizes or represents Jose Rizal during the Spanish Colonization. Deguzman, J. (2020). Noli Me Tangere Characters. https://www.artstation.com/artwork/486myY
Kapitan Tiyago Kapitan Tiyago was a rich and influential businessman in San Diego who was born and raised in the Philippines. He was the one who raised Maria Clara and is the husband of Doña Pia Alba. He was the closest friend of both Padre Damaso and Padre Salvi. He symbolizes power, greed and how privileged people like him back then were the ones to abuse their power and people. Delos Santos, D. (2022). New Haircut. https://twitter.com/donsantiagodel2/status/1221089039807213568?lang=zh-Hant
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso was a Franciscan friar and was the former priest in San Diego. He was best known for speaking with harsh words and has been very cruel all throughout his stay in San Diego. Although Maria Clara was raised by Kapitan Tiyago, her real father is Padre Damaso for he sexually assaulted Doña Pia Alba. He was also one of the closest friends of Kapitan Tiyago. Padre Damaso represents the typical friars back then who were greedy, abusive and hungry of power. N/A. (2022). Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Padre_Damaso
